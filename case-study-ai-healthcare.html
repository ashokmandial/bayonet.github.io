Case Study

AI-Powered Healthcare Diagnostics

Industry: Healthcare • Duration: 8 Months

The Challenge

A leading regional hospital network was facing a significant backlog in radiology image analysis. The manual review process was time-consuming, prone to human fatigue, and delayed critical patient diagnoses, ultimately affecting patient outcomes.

Our Solution

Bayonet Technologies partnered with the hospital's radiology team to develop a custom AI-driven diagnostic tool. We trained a deep learning computer vision model using thousands of anonymized historical scans to identify subtle anomalies, such as early-stage tumors and micro-fractures, with high precision.

The Implementation

The solution was seamlessly integrated into their existing PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication System). Rather than replacing radiologists, the AI acted as a "second set of eyes," automatically flagging high-risk scans for immediate human review and prioritizing the daily workflow.

The Results

  • 40% Reduction in average scan review time.
  • 98.5% Accuracy in detecting early-stage anomalies, matching top specialists.
  • Zero Backlog: The hospital cleared its 3-week backlog within the first two months of deployment.
