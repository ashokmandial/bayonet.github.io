Case Study

IoT Supply Chain Optimization

Industry: Manufacturing • Duration: 10 Months

The Challenge

A massive manufacturing firm experienced frequent supply chain disruptions due to inventory mismanagement. They lacked visibility into their stock levels across multiple regional warehouses, leading to overstocking in some areas and critical shortages in others.

Our Solution

We designed an Internet of Things (IoT) driven supply chain network. By outfitting key inventory pallets and storage bays with smart sensors, we created a system that monitored stock levels, temperature, and movement in real-time.

The Implementation

The sensor data was aggregated and piped into a secure cloud environment via AWS IoT Core. We built a central command dashboard for logistics managers that offered predictive analytics, alerting them to reorder parts automatically before a shortage could halt the production line.

The Results

  • 15% Reduction in overall inventory holding costs.
  • Production Halt Elimination: Achieved a 99.9% uptime for the manufacturing assembly line.
  • Global Visibility: Managers gained a unified, real-time view of inventory across 14 facilities worldwide.
